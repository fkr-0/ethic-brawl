# Aristotle

## Short Bio
System-builder of logic and virtue ethics; balanced all-round fighter with grounded stance control.

## Visual + Animation Guidelines
- White robe with bronze trim, scroll strap, short staff.
- Breathing idle: chest expansion and staff hand micro-adjustments.
- Walk: calm procession pace with staff taps.
- Run: robe folds trail behind; deliberate but forceful stride.
- Run attack should read as a phalanx-like thrust.
- Run+jump attack should be compact and disciplined, not acrobatic.

## Special Attacks (5)
1. Golden Mean Jab (`D + ATK`): fast two-hit poke, low startup.
2. Syllogism Lance (`D + F + ATK`): piercing linear thrust.
3. Categoric Guard (`D + B + ATK`): brief armor window with pushback.
4. Lyceum Wheel (`RUN + ATK`): advancing spin-strike.
5. Prime Mover Drop (`RUN + JMP + ATK`): overhead falling strike with guard break.
