# Focault (Michel Foucault)

## Short Bio
French theorist of institutions, discipline, and social control; fights with evasive footwork and control-zone pressure.

## Visual + Animation Guidelines
- Slim silhouette, bald head, black turtleneck under long coat; hand gesture like tracing diagrams in air.
- Breathing idle: shoulders rise subtly, coat hem oscillates.
- Walk: measured stalking steps, minimal upper-body sway.
- Run: forward lean with coat flare, sharp braking pose.
- Run attack should feel like a sudden “containment rush”.
- Run+jump attack should arc diagonally with a descending stamp.

## Special Attacks (6)
1. Panopticon Arc (`D + ATK`): short-range circular strike that marks enemy for chip damage.
2. Archive Burst (`D + F + ATK`): mid-range ink-wave projectile.
3. Discipline Break (`D + B + ATK`): counter stance; reflects one frontal hit.
4. Biopower Lash (`JMP + ATK`): aerial downward whip strike.
5. Corridor Sweep (`RUN + ATK`): shoulder rush into two-hit baton combo.
6. Tower Drop (`RUN + JMP + ATK`): leap-in descending smash with small shock ring.
