# Mikhail Bakunin

## Short Bio
Chaotic grappler with explosive entries, chain pressure, and high-risk mobility.

## Visual + Animation Guidelines
- Wild beard/hair, worker coat, chain-wrapped forearms.
- Breathing idle: twitchy shoulder motion and chain sway.
- Walk: restless side-to-side weight shifts.
- Run: aggressive sprint with wide arm swing.
- Run attack should be abrupt and unruly.
- Run+jump attack should be a reckless diving crash.

## Special Attacks (6)
1. Uprising Elbow (`D + ATK`): close elbow with stagger.
2. Barricade Toss (`D + F + ATK`): throws debris projectile.
3. Null Throne (`D + B + ATK`): break-grab counter.
4. Riot Kick (`JMP + ATK`): forward spinning aerial kick.
5. Black Flag Rush (`RUN + ATK`): running grab into slam.
6. Anarchy Drop (`RUN + JMP + ATK`): diving body press with bounce.
