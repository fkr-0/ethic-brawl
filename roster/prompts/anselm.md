# Anselm of Canterbury

## Short Bio
Monastic duelist whose precise timing and faith-infused counters convert defense into offense.

## Visual + Animation Guidelines
- Monastic robes, narrow silhouette, illuminated manuscript talisman.
- Breathing idle: prayer-hand cadence with robe flutter.
- Walk: restrained gliding motion.
- Run: robe ribboning with compact steps.
- Run attack should be a quick piercing lunge.
- Run+jump attack should combine prayer gesture and impact flare.

## Special Attacks (5)
1. Proslogion Pierce (`D + ATK`): fast thrust with bonus vs blocking.
2. Ontological Ray (`D + F + ATK`): straight holy beam.
3. Canterbury Reversal (`D + B + ATK`): parry into knockback pulse.
4. Cloister Dash (`RUN + ATK`): forward stab and retreat hop.
5. Credo Dive (`RUN + JMP + ATK`): diving strike with light stun.
